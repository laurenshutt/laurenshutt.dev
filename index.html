About me Skills Download résumé

Hi, I’m Lauren. 👋🏻

I'm a self-taught full-stack developer, focusing on front-end design and development. My academic background is in anthropology, which means I care just as much about the people and Weberian webs of significance behind the website as I do about writing clean code for the front end. In practice, it also means I build thoughtful, well-structured digital experiences that balance clarity with personality. I’m love incorporating subtle microinteractions and dynamic visuals to create experiences that feel intuitive, engaging, and a little playful. 🌿

Outside of work, you’ll usually find me playing Stardew Valley, building Notion templates, crafting, reading, or being closely supervised by two cats and a pittski who believe they manage this entire operation. 🐾
